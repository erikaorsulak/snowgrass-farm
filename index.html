About the Farm

Snowgrass Farm is a 1.5-acre diversified produce farm located a dozen miles north of Leavenworth, Washington, in the foothills of the Central Cascades.

Our Farm-Fresh Produce

This year's offerings will include everything from greens to root vegetables to herbs, and much, much more. Find us at the Leavenworth and Plain Farmer's Markets and on-site at the farm.
